MODULE: User Experience

BRIEF DESCRIPTION:

For this module, we were put into groups and tasked to choose a service/product, and recreate either their website, microsite, EDM, or Kiosk.
I chose to do website, and using the techniques I learnt over the course of the module, redesigned Gardens by the Bays' main website. My design
was finalised after creating various prototypes that were tested by other people.
